Robert Trent

Classical Guitarist

"...a sense of structural integrity that informs every passing tone.”

NEW YORK TIMES


“A SEASONED ARTIST"

-PHILADELPHIA INQUIRER


From Bangkok to Rio de Janeiro, from Paris to Milan, from Saigon to Miami, Robert Trent continually captivates audiences in performances and recordings ranging from his own compositions, exciting premiers of world-renowned composers; critics and audiences alike laud his exquisite tonal palette, attention to detail, form, and exciting energetic performances in solo and duets with fortepiano and period guitars.
